About Imbrace

Imbrace is a fashionable line for women with low back pain.

The journey of Imbrace began with one person's desire to dress well while easing her back pain. Refusing to wear ugly lumbar support belts, Imbrace was born. Imbrace is about maintaining the balance between style and comfort.

You can style yourself by creating customized products that are made-to-fit. Bridging customization and personalization, Imbrace aims at reinvogorating the traditional practice of tailoring in a modern day setup.
